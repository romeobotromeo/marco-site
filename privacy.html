Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

Marco by JWGRE, a service operated by Gibson House LLC ("Marco," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our SMS-based website management service.

Information We Collect

When you use Marco, we collect:

  • Phone Number: Your mobile phone number, used to communicate with you about your website.
  • Message Content: The text messages you send us containing your website update requests.
  • Website Content: Information you provide for your website (business name, photos, contact details, etc.).

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide our website management service
  • Process your website update requests
  • Communicate with you about your account and service
  • Send you service-related messages (confirmations, updates, support)

We do not share your mobile phone number or any mobile information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Access to customer data is strictly limited to authorized personnel

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you cancel your account, we delete your personal information within 30 days, unless required by law to retain it.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your information
  • Opt-out: Stop receiving messages at any time by texting STOP

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Marco:

  • Twilio: SMS messaging infrastructure
  • Stripe: Payment processing

These providers have their own privacy policies and handle data according to their terms.

Children's Privacy

Marco is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by sending a text message to your registered phone number.
